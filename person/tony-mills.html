Managing Director Pantera Wardrobes & PG Doors

Tony
MILLS

Managing Director Pantera Wardrobes & PG Doors

Tony Joined Pantera when Pantera Carpentry had recently completed some initial wardrobe supply and installation packages, which were delivered by importing wardrobes in a flat pack style from Italy. Tony has a construction industry background, firstly undertaking a building services apprenticeship with Matthew Hall Engineering, followed by time working in an architects practice and finally completed twelve years at WSP as a services co-ordinator draughtsman.

Since joining Pantera, Tony has been integral to the growth and development of Pantera Wardrobes by designing the UK built Urbanmobili front frame wardrobe ranges and other products such as bathroom cabinets and vanity units.

During his 10 years at Pantera Tony has undertaken extensive training in business management and Health and Safety, he has gained a wealth of experience in operations and can deliver product from early design to a scheme of several hundred units.

Now as Managing Director for Pantera Wardrobes, Tony has a vision that will see the continued business expansion and development of new concepts and exciting designs, that will add further to the business’s current portfolio offerings.

Tony leads design in the group with his team supporting all divisions with product development and design.

"I’m proud of how the wardrobes business has developed, it still excites me to take an initial concept design, see it develop and deliver the finished article to site. Being supported by a great team that makes this happen, is also very rewarding"

Key Roles:

  • Managing director Pantera Wardrobes

  • Sales and Marketing Pantera Wardrobes

  • Commercial lead Pantera Wardrobes

  • Special Design Projects
