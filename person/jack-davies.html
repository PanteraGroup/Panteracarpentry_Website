DAVIES

Contracts Manager London

Jack joined the business in 2016 as a Senior Quantity Surveyor, where he played a key role as the commercial lead on several flagship schemes. In 2018, Jack’s responsibilities expanded to include complete oversight of the 10 Park Drive project in Canary Wharf, managing all operations and commercial aspects of the project.

Since then, Jack has led several significant schemes for the Pantera Group and is now also contributing to the development of business practices as the company works towards achieving Net Zero Carbon. Prior to joining Pantera, Jack worked on UK and international projects in the foundations sector. He is a Chartered Quantity Surveyor and holds a diploma in Adjudication

. 

"The construction industry is a highly talented sector that is built off the hard work, dedication and innovation of Specialist Contractors. I’m driven to ensure Specialist Contractors get the recognition they deserve for the pivotal role they play when delivering construction projects. I was first attracted to Pantera as they were an exciting company that was becoming a key player in the carpentry and joinery sector. Since then, the business has developed into a foothold in the joinery market in London & South East and I am excited to help showcase Pantera’s standards beyond expectation on our journey to be the number one Specialist Contractor for any construction client."

Key Role:

  • Lead for Major Projects

  • Operations and Delivery

  • Lead for Net Zero Carbon

  • Management of London Construction Teams
