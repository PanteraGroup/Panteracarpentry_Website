PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy and security are important to you, important to us and we take them seriously.

This privacy policy explains how we collect data and use it in order to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation 2018 (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 1998 (DPA). This website complies with both the DPA and the GDPR.

Handling Your Information

Any personal information that you have provided to us (your name, email address, phone numbers and any other information) will be gathered, transported securely and held securely by us.

Google Analytics

When someone visits our website, we use a third-party service called Google Analytics to collect information about visitor behaviour patterns. We do this so we can understand how visitors make use of the website and so we can improve its content and flow. The information is available to us in only an aggregated format and we are not able to identify any individuals from the data that Google Analytics collects. IP addresses are anonymised.

Cookies Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer which are used to enhance the experience when you browse the site and improve the way the site works. By accepting the cookie notice on this site, you are accepting the use of cookies whilst using the website. You are able to block all cookies from our website by changing your browser settings but this may affect functionality.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is kept under regular review. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at info@panteracarpentry.com.
