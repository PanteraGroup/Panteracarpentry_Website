OUR FUTURE

Over 400+ apprentices trained since 1998, and proudly
supporting the Armed Forces Covenant since 2020.

Over the past 25 years we have taken hundreds of young people and trained them as apprentices to become fully qualified carpenters.
We also train apprentices and graduates to become supervisors, project managers, estimators, quantity surveyors and designers.
Our goal is to help young people, putting them in the best position possible to start their career in the construction industry.
