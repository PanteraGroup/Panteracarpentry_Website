SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

As a company, Pantera offers a wide range of services at various levels. We can handle complete fit-outs, providing a turnkey package, or we can simply supply trained, skilled staff for the installation of a client's product based on their designs.

With a proven track record of delivering high-quality results and repeat business from most of our clients, we have built a reputation for excellence. In the South East, our skilled workforce specialises in housing projects, offering services that range from roofing and first-fix installations to complete second-fix services, including stairs, bathrooms, and kitchens. Our experienced Supervisors, Managers, and Directors bring decades of expertise to every project. We also provide listing services for trusses, timber frame structures, and cladding, all carried out by our own skilled and qualified staff.

MANUFACTURING

We have over 15,000 sq ft of manufacturing and assembly space across 2 facilities with the latest Homag woodworking machines for manufacturing wardrobes, bathrooms and doors. We can provide packages where our products are used, providing savings by reducing the number of companies in the supply chain. We also sell all our products for your own teams to install. We understand construction sites and installation and have the knowledge to ensure all our products are suitable for quick and simple delivery, distribution and fitting.

FLEXIBILITY

No two jobs are the same. We are never tied up in processes that mean we cannot be flexible. We can work on making products unique to your projects' exact requirements. We always try our best to meet our clients' needs on every project while supporting with early engagement on mock-up apartments, testing product designs and sales and marketing suites.
