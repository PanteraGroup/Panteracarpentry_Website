Sustainability

OUR COMMITMENT

We endeavour to incorporate environmental, social, and governance criteria in all of our operations. Reflecting our commitment to our clients, employees, contractors, supply chain and the communities which we operate. ESG is a continuous process aligning our operations and practices with our values and vision as a company.

ESG is embedded in our services, our products, our people and supply chain, our environment and our corporate governance.

Our Services and Products

Since we were founded in 1998 we have provided services to the built environment and since 2006 we have also manufactured products for this sector. Working in construction providing carpenters and joiners and related trades we strive to deliver our work to the highest standard. Delivering over 7,500 homes in the South East and over 15,000 apartments in London as well as schools, resident facilities, offices, gyms and much more. All of our work is compliant with the laws, regulations and best practices for our industry.

People and Supply Chain Partners

Pantera is built by our dedicated employees and their valued contributions to the success of the company. Supported by our supply chain partners that make it possible for us to do what we do. We are committed to equal rights and opportunity that's easily seen by the diversity within the company. Our employees created our core values and vision as a business to ensure we deliver to the highest standard, every time. We engage with our clients and supply chain to ensure we only work with those who have aligned ethos and values; creating a positive and engaging working environment. We offer competitive salaries, training and development opportunities, cultural development and initiative programmes to positively impact all who work for us.

Our Environment
(Net Zero commitment)

Pantera is committed to initiatives that minimise the environmental impact of our work and are working towards Net Zero Carbon by understanding our baseline and the carbon we produce and setting scientifically based targets for reduction. We are partnering with our clients committed to reducing carbon produced in the built environment. We have electric vehicles and are continually replacing old vehicles with new electric ones. We are moving towards renewable energy for our offices and manufacturing. We recycle and procure recycled products, as well as ensuring wood products come from sustainable sources with the accreditations to prove our supplier chain of custody.

Corporate Governance

Pantera is proud of its corporate governance policies that operate across all parts of the business. All employees, directors, managers, supply chain and contractors are required to act with integrity, honesty and the highest standard of ethical conduct. We encourage and support the ongoing development of director and management in the business to ensure this is part of our culture, building honest relationships with everyone the business interacts with.
