THE SCHEME

Situated on the southern edge of Paddock Wood, this brand new development comprises a collection of 3 and 4 bedroom houses. Benefiting from an attractive countryside setting, yet within walking distance to Paddock Wood town centre, schools, retail, sporting facilities and the train station with frequent connections reaching London Bridge in just 41 minutes.
